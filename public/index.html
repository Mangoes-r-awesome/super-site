The Beginning

From a student's suggestion to 20,000+ guests

The business started when a former student turned friend casually suggested we convert our home into an Airbnb. That one property became five. Five became twenty. Today, Super has hosted over 20,000 guests across five geographies.

Along the way, we built something most property managers don't have: a full-service operational engine powered by technology, run by real people who actually care.
